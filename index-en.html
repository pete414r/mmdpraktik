Methods and portfolio



Media Design!


The study has provided insight about quality, use of visual effects in a genre chosen design. Adobe cloud and other relevant software especially from Google chrome shop has been used doing the digital work. Insight on digital design is relevant, how do we shape it in interaction with others. The media flow is fast "here and now". It requires an understanding of the hardware and the social aspects, especially when the deadline must be held.



Evaluation & Future space!


Multimedia Design studio has offered many exciting challenges. The largest has been the animation Planning Association, aimed at an older audience and the nationwide campaign for young abstainers. The essential insight has been the importance of empirical data collection and testing of the solution before delivery. It requires a wide range of knowledge in all areas, from front-end setup to work with raportage and visual expression in high fidelity.

Theme and CV



Company


Create maximum added value for the customer. It's all about selection of target groups, business partners, production facilities, channels and relationships. Modeles is used to develop situation and competition analysis from the strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats.





Digital content


Digital content is often the choice of media, communications, action plan and how to energise the best possible change of target group behavior. The completion and the final solution is found using design thinking together with the clients interests and acceptance of product from brief to finished product.





User Experience


The best solution is developed using empiricism collection from desk to field research. The selection is taken to tested for errors in coding, content and design. Layout is chosen based on studies of how different devices are interacted.





User Interface


Recognizability, structure and language must seem attractive to the user. Here is the genre, styles and use of language important. Is the site static or dynamic, has it using role login in a content management system (CMS) or is it a eas(e)/(y) mobil sulution with google maps routing directions between to selected points.
